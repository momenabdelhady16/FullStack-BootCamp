Developer's Journey

Game Description:
Developer's Journey is a unique RPG experience that relies entirely on logical and programming puzzles. The game takes place inside a giant motherboard, where the player acts as a systems engineer trying to fix corrupted codes and fight viruses to reach the main processor.


Audio Design:
To enhance focus, the game is completely devoid of music, relying instead on ambient white noise and developer voiceovers, with precise mechanical sound effects when interacting with system components.
